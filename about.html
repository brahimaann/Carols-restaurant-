Our Story

Our Heritage

Welcome to Carol's

Nestled in the heart of Brooklyn Center, Minnesota, Carol's Restaurant is more than just a place to eat — it's a celebration of Liberian culture, tradition, and the warmth of home-cooked food.

Every dish on our menu is rooted in the rich culinary heritage of West Africa. From our slow-simmered cassava leaves to our perfectly seasoned jollof rice, each recipe has been passed down through generations and prepared with the same love and care that brings families together.

At Carol's, we believe food is a bridge — connecting cultures, building community, and creating memories that last a lifetime. Whether you're a first-time visitor curious about Liberian cuisine or a lifelong fan craving the flavors of home, you'll always find a warm welcome and a full plate here.
